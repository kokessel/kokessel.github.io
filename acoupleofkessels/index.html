Our Story

Thursday, September 9, 2011 will be remembered by most as the first day of Tropical Storm Lee, a day when many roads in South Eastern PA and Delaware were flooded making travel nearly impossible. However, Ashley and Kory will always remember it as the day they first began talking.

Stuck in the cramped backseat of her father’s car, Ashley was on her way to be the maid of honor at her Aunt’s wedding. What should have been a short drive to Delaware, turned into an hours long commute through a maze of flooded streets. Bored, she cheered when the text message from Kory popped up on her phone. A conversation originally full of small talk banter, turned into a lengthy discussion, and finally a date to have lunch together when Millersville University reopened.

As the days passed and the two enjoyed one another’s company over cafeteria food, Kory decided to ask Ashley out to dinner and a movie. By October 14, the two began officially dating.

Five years, 2 graduations, 1 apartment, and a Pickle (their dog) later, Kory asked Ashley to be his wife.
